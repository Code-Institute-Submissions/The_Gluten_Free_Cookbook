Our Mission

Our Mission for this community is to connect, share and cook recipes that are catered towards the gluten free diet. Created by our founders April and Toby Thompson we wanted to ensure that being on a gluten free diet should not be a restriction. We are here for you! We love food and cooking great food. Being Gluten Free should not stop us from cooking the food we love!

Our Founders
April & Toby Thompson

Welcome to The Gluten Free Cookbook! Our online community dedicated to food lovers that prefer or are restricted to a gluten free diet. Almost 3 years ago, my husband had to be put on a gluten free diet. We were heartbroken, we love food and love cooking food. We felt restricted to what we were able to make and eat. But, we then discovered that being on a gluten free diet should not restrict us, but forces us to be more creative on the food choices we make. So, welcome to this online community. We want to share and discover recipes from backgrounds that are gluten free. Let's Get Cooking!
